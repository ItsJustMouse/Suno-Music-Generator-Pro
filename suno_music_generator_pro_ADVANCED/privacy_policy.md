# Privacy Policy - Suno Music Generator Pro

**Effective Date:** November 2024  
**Last Updated:** November 2024

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Suno Music Generator Pro ("we," "our," or "the Service") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our GPT-powered songwriting service optimized for all Suno AI model versions.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 User-Provided Information
- **Song Details:** Titles, genres, themes, and creative preferences you provide
- **Conversation Data:** Your interactions with the GPT during song creation
- **Feedback:** Any ratings or feedback you provide about generated songs

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the service
- **Session Information:** Timestamps and duration of sessions
- **Technical Data:** Browser type, device information (anonymized)

### 2.3 What We DO NOT Collect
- Personal identification information (unless voluntarily provided)
- Payment information (handled by OpenAI/third parties)
- Location data
- Biometric data

## 3. How We Use Information

### 3.1 Primary Uses
- Generate customized songs based on your inputs
- Improve song generation quality and accuracy
- Provide technical support
- Maintain service functionality

### 3.2 Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
- Train and improve our song generation algorithms
- Develop new genres and sub-genres based on demand

## 4. Information Sharing

### 4.1 We DO NOT:
- Sell your information to third parties
- Share your creative content without permission
- Use your songs for commercial purposes
- Share data with advertisers

### 4.2 We MAY Share Information:
- With Suno AI (only formatted song data for generation)
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect rights, safety, or property
- With your explicit consent

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Storage
- Conversation data is stored temporarily during sessions
- Generated songs may be cached for performance (24 hours)
- No permanent storage of personal information

### 5.2 Security Measures
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits
- Compliance with OpenAI's security standards

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 You Can:
- Request deletion of conversation history
- Opt-out of data collection for improvement
- Access information we have about you
- Correct inaccurate information

### 6.2 How to Exercise Rights
Contact us through the GPT interface or at: privacy@suno-redux.ai

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 8. International Users

Data may be processed in the United States. By using our service, you consent to transfer and processing of data in accordance with this policy.

## 9. Third-Party Services

### 9.1 OpenAI
This GPT operates on OpenAI's platform. Your use is also subject to OpenAI's privacy policy and terms of service.

### 9.2 Suno AI
